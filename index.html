Relaxing Rivers We Run

We believe that there is nothing like a river trip for relaxation. That's why we subscribe to the dry oar philosophy of boating. Keeping your oars dry to us means taking time to look around and notice the beauty that surrounds the rivers we raft and love.

Now, that's not to say we don't also like some rapids!

When it comes to real white water rapids, we don't even try to keep that oar dry; we get it as wet as we can. Dry Oar believes that the tenseness that comes when you are looking down the tongue of a big string of waves is something worth living for, as well as also makes you appreciate even more the calm beautiful water that always follows even. Opposites attract, don't they?

Come Join the Fun!

Whether you are coming to rest on the serenity of clam lakes, or get restless on the rivers of white water mayhem, we encourage you to come have the "Dry Oar" experience.
